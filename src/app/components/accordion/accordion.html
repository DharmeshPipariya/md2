Accordion(Md2Accordion)

{{tab.content}}

The accordion component builds on top of the collapse directive to provide a list of items, with collapsible bodies that are collapsed or expanded by clicking on the item's header.

The body of each accordion group is transcluded into the body of the collapsible element.

md2-accordion settings

  • multiple (Default: false) - Control whether expanding an item will cause the other items to close.

  • md-class (Default: none) - Add ability to set classes your own. This must be a string.

  • open Event - It fires after a open tab.

  • close Event - It fires after a close tab.

md2-accordion-tab settings

  • header (Default: none) - The clickable text on the tab's header. You need one to be able to click on the header for toggling.

  • disabled (Default: false) - Whether the accordion tab is disabled or not.

  • active (Default: false) - Whether accordion tab is open or closed.
